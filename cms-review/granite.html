Granite Countertops

Granite countertop estimation is based on the total square footage of countertop surface area. Measurements are taken from the interior construct in AutoCAD. You can also refer to the floorplan in the PDF plan drawing for assistance.

How to Measure

Use color-coded polylines to draw rectangles around each counter or vanity top.
*Insert 'Granite CAD' image here*

What to Include

  • Kitchen countertops/Misc. areas like laundry and mudrooms - Red
  • Bathroom vanity tops - Yellow
  • Specialty granite like outdoor kitchens - Magenta

Estimation

  • Copy your polylines off to the side and keep a set on the plan.
  • Enter the sum of the area of the red rectangles into the SFT of Kitchen Countertop section of the estimating spreadsheet.
  • Enter the sum of the area of the yellow rectangles into the SFT of Vanity Top.
*Insert 'Granite XL' image here*
  • With the rectangles still left on the plan, explode lines into regular lines.
  • Remove all lines that are not touching a wall.
  • Pull out the remaining lines and get the linear footage, then convert to linear inches. 
*Insert 'Backsplash CAD' image here*
  • Place total linear inches of the red lines in the Kitchen 4” splash section.
  • Place total linear inches of the yellow lines in the Vanity 4” splash section.
The total of the red and yellow lines after being exploded and adjusted are to be converted to linear inches before being inputted into the splash sections.

Tip

Include sinks, exclude appliances like refrigerators and stoves. (Move this note before the 'Estimation' section.)
